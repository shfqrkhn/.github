# Code of Conduct

Be respectful, specific, and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Assume good intent.
- Keep feedback focused on the project and user impact.
- Share enough evidence for others to reproduce or evaluate the issue.
- Respect privacy and do not post secrets, private data, or harassment.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Spam, unrelated promotion, or repeated low-effort noise.
- Publishing private information or exploit details in public threads.

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that harms project quality, safety, or maintainability.
